          About  YKR

🐟 Welcome to YKR  Boneless Fish  and Prawns  Distributor

At YKR Fish and prawn Distributor, we bring you the freshest, finest, and most sustainable boneless fish and prawns straight from trusted sources to your table. Whether you’re a restaurant, retailer, or home buyer, we ensure that every catch is handled with care and delivered with quality you can trust.

                                                                                                                          Our services

Large size 

Enjoy the perfect blend of taste, nutrition, and convenience with our premium Large Size Boneless Fish. Carefully cleaned, expertly cut, and completely free from bones, this fish is ideal for families, restaurants, and catering services.

Medium size 

Our Medium-Sized Boneless Fish is the perfect choice for those who love fresh, tender, and flavorful seafood without the hassle of bones. Each piece is carefully processed, making it quick to cook and easy to enjoy.

Cut pieces

Enjoy the convenience of ready-to-cook cut pieces of boneless fish—perfectly portioned for quick meals without the hassle of cleaning or cutting. Each piece is tender, fresh, and bone-free, making it an excellent choice for both traditional and modern dishes.
